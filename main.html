Sling Bags

Are they cool? Or do they only look good on models in very specific circumstances?

We went out and did some extensive testing in order to find out.

Initial Questioning

We have sent out a questionnaire to experts in their own field asking them about what they think about sling bags without being given any extra information in the forms of pictures or advertisements. The views, as expected, were split. Some were saying that they are pretty awesome and an invaluable item to have on you in a new city when you dont want to haul around a large backpack, and some have just simply said that the bags look homosexual. The third stance was that, since bags come in all sorts of shapes and sizes and styles, some do indeed look homosexual, while others look fairly arousing. Sexy, as the kids say nowadays.

Further Testing

Our experts have taken to their closets to find the bags that they already own in order to test if they are indeed "drippy" in certain situations.
